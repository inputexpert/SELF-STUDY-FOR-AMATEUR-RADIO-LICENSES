## Technician Class: Introduction
This is the first module in your study path. It provides an overview of the amateur radio service, the core regulations governing the Technician license, and the fundamental technical concepts required to pass the FCC examination.
### Learning Objectives
 * Understand the purpose and basis of the amateur radio service.
 * Identify the scope of operating privileges granted by a Technician class license.
 * Establish a foundational understanding of basic radio theory and safety protocols.
### Study Path
 1. **Regulations & Rules**: Familiarizing yourself with FCC Part 97.
 2. **Operating Procedures**: Understanding band plans and communication protocols.
 3. **Radio Fundamentals**: Basic electronics, propagation, and antenna systems.
