## Technician Class: Operating Procedures
This module focuses on the practical application of radio communications, encompassing standard protocols, band plans, and proper station etiquette.
### Key Concepts
 * **Band Plans**: Understanding the voluntary frequency allocations for various modes of operation within the Technician bands.
 * **Communication Protocols**: Mastering the use of call signs, phonetic alphabets, and common "Q-codes" for efficient information exchange.
 * **Station Etiquette**: Applying best practices for net operations, repeater usage, and interference management.
### Learning Objectives
 * Demonstrate correct station identification procedures according to FCC requirements.
 * Interpret and utilize band plans to select appropriate operating frequencies.
 * Execute effective voice and data communications using standard industry conventions.
