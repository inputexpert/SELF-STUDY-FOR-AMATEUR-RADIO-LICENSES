## Technician Class: Regulatory Basis
This module covers the legal framework of Amateur Radio in the United States, primarily focusing on FCC Part 97 rules and regulations.
### Key Concepts
 * **Basis and Purpose**: Understanding the five principles of the Amateur Radio Service as defined by the FCC.
 * **Licensing System**: The structure of the amateur licensing tiers and the privileges afforded to a Technician licensee.
 * **Control Operator**: Identifying who is responsible for the operation of an amateur station and the legal requirements for station identification.
### Learning Objectives
 * Define the amateur service and its primary objectives.
 * Recognize the Technician class operating frequencies and modes.
 * Master the rules for station identification and international communication.
