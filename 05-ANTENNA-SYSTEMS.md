# 05-ANTENNA-SYSTEMS.md
## Technician Class: Antenna Systems
This module covers the physical and electrical design of antennas and feedlines, focusing on how to efficiently radiate and receive signals.
### Key Concepts
 * **Antenna Types**: Understanding the characteristics of dipole, vertical, and directional antennas.
 * **Feedlines and SWR**: The role of coaxial cable, impedance matching, and the importance of maintaining a low Standing Wave Ratio (SWR).
 * **Safety Protocols**: Essential safety practices regarding RF exposure limits and the physical installation of antenna structures.
### Learning Objectives
 * Compare the radiation patterns and applications of common amateur antennas.
 * Explain the function of an antenna tuner and why SWR is a critical measurement.
 * Implement best practices for grounding and lightning protection in an antenna system.
