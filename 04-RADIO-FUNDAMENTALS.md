# 04-RADIO-FUNDAMENTALS.md
## Technician Class: Radio Fundamentals
This module bridges the gap between theoretical physics and practical hardware, focusing on the electrical principles that enable amateur radio communication.
### Key Concepts
 * **Electrical Principles**: Understanding voltage, current, resistance, and power using Ohm’s Law and the Watt’s Law relationship.
 * **Component Theory**: Identifying the functions of resistors, capacitors, inductors, and diodes in a radio circuit.
 * **Wave Propagation**: Exploring how radio signals travel through the atmosphere and the effects of frequency on propagation.
### Learning Objectives
 * Calculate basic circuit values using Ohm’s Law.
 * Identify the schematic symbols for common electronic components.
 * Explain the relationship between frequency, wavelength, and signal velocity.
