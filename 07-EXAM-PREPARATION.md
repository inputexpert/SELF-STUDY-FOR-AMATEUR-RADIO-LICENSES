# 07-EXAM-PREPARATION.md
## Technician Class: Exam Preparation
This final module provides strategic guidance and resources to ensure readiness for the FCC Technician Class licensing examination.
### Key Concepts
 * **Question Pool Analysis**: Understanding the structure, categories, and frequency of topics within the current NCVEC question pool.
 * **Study Techniques**: Implementing active recall and spaced repetition methodologies to retain technical information and regulations.
 * **Testing Logistics**: Requirements for exam registration, locating Volunteer Examiner (VE) sessions, and understanding the scoring criteria.
### Learning Objectives
 * Successfully navigate the current FCC Technician question pool.
 * Utilize effective practice testing strategies to identify knowledge gaps.
 * Complete the necessary steps to register for and pass the licensing examination.
