## Technician Class: Safety and Equipment
This module covers the practical aspects of station setup, hardware maintenance, and rigorous safety standards required for operating an amateur radio station.
### Key Concepts
 * **Station Setup**: Essential hardware including transceivers, power supplies, and proper grounding techniques.
 * **RF Safety**: Understanding MPE (Maximum Permissible Exposure) limits, duty cycles, and maintaining a safe station environment.
 * **Emergency Preparedness**: Protocols for station operation during public service events or emergency situations.
### Learning Objectives
 * Identify the primary components required for a functional amateur radio station.
 * Implement effective grounding and bonding to ensure equipment safety and noise reduction.
 * Demonstrate knowledge of safe operating distances and exposure limits for various power levels and antenna configurations.
